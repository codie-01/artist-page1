RYAN
INGLIS

GUITARIST/SINGER-SONGWRITER

Ryan Inglis is a British guitarist/singer-songwriter based in Munich, Germany.

His musical style is inspired by Newton Faulkner, Radiohead, Ed Sheeran, and John Mayer. He combines acoustic guitar with percussive elements and has a distinctive, country-like voice. Known for his original songs and unique covers, he captivates audiences with his dynamic performances.
